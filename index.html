ManualNest

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

ManualNest is an offline-first app that helps you keep owner manuals, warranty details, receipts, model and serial numbers, parts, and maintenance schedules for the things you own.

The short version. Offline. No account. No tracking. Everything you save stays on your device. ManualNest doesn't include analytics, advertising, or telemetry. We don't make network calls unless you tap a manual URL you saved yourself.

Data We Collect

ManualNest does not require an account and does not collect personal data on our servers. The app stores all of your data locally on your device. This can include item names, brands, model and serial numbers, rooms, purchase dates, prices, store names, warranty expiry dates, parts notes, filter and battery sizes, maintenance intervals, troubleshooting notes, and any general notes you enter.

Photos, Receipts, and PDFs

If you choose to attach a photo, receipt image, or manual PDF, ManualNest stores it inside the app's private folder on your device. The camera and photo picker are used only for the file you select. These files remain on your device unless you export or share them yourself.

Network Calls

ManualNest does not make network calls in the course of normal use. The only exception is when you tap a manual URL that you yourself saved against an item — opening that link hands off to your device's default browser. That open action is initiated by you and is governed by that browser's privacy practices.

Analytics, Tracking, and Ads

ManualNest does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking, or cross-app tracking. There is no telemetry built into the app.

Exports and Sharing

You can export your saved items as JSON. You can also share an item summary as a PDF via your device's share sheet. Any exported or shared file is controlled by you and may contain the item details you entered, including model and serial numbers.

Permissions

If you choose to use camera capture for labels, barcodes, or receipts, ManualNest uses your camera through the system camera app or via your device's standard permission flow. The app does not declare any background data or location permissions.

Children

ManualNest is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.

ManualNest is an offline-first household vault. This policy describes what the app does on your device and what it doesn't do off it.
